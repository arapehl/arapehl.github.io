The nostalgia I have for the good ol&#8217; days of the [demo scene][1] was rekindled&#8211;if not set ablaze&#8211;today by [Christian Heilmann][2] who pointed me to a recent release by [Farbrausch][3] called &#8220;[Debris][4]&#8220;. In my opinion it&#8217;s one of the best demos ever. You can [download][5] and run the Windows executable from pouet.net, or you can [watch a recorded version of it on YouTube][6].

When watching it though, keep one thing in mind. The **entire** demo has a tiny 177kb footprint. That&#8217;s right, **177kb** (whereas the [low res .avi][7] is 164MB and the [high res .mp4][8] is 197MB). Man I love the demo scene.
